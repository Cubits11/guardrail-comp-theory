\section{Foundations and Motivation}
We study binary rails $A,B\in\{0,1\}$ and class labels $Y\in\{0,1\}$ with $Y=1$ denoting harmful
content. Compositions such as logical AND/OR are evaluated under unknown dependence constrained only by
observed marginals. The goal is to obtain certifiable, adversarially-robust safety guarantees and to
optimise guardrail policies subject to stakeholder utilities.
